About

ResolveIt is your one-stop online platform to connect with your government, have your say, and make a difference. We believe every citizen's voice deserves to be heard, and this AI-powered system makes it easier than ever to share your feedback and report issues.

Features

Interactive Chat-bot

Ask questions, report issues, and share feedback with the government.

Complaints Tracking

Get real-time updates on the status of your escalated complaints.

Sentiment Analysis

Gain insights into citizens' opinions and concerns about government services.

Recommendation Engine

Get actionable recommendations for improving government services based on sentiment analysis.

Data Visualization

Analyze trends and patterns in citizen feedback through clear and interactive visualizations.

How it Works

User Registration and Authentication

User starts by registering on the website and providing necessary details for authentication. This ensures that only authorized users can access the system.

Chat-bot Interaction

Authorized users can then ask questions, report issues, or provide feedback through the chat-bot interface. The chat-bot employs Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand and respond to user queries in real-time.

Follow-up of Escalated Complaints

Critical issues are identified and escalated, triggering alerts for immediate attention. Automated follow-up mechanisms keep the users informed about the status of their complaints.

Sentiment Analysis

User interactions, including chat logs, issue reports, and feedback, undergo sentiment analysis. The system gauges the sentiment of citizens to understand public perception.

Recommendation System

Based on sentiment analysis results, the recommendation system suggests actions or improvements to the government. Proactive recommendations help address specific areas of concern.

Data Visualization

Data visualization tools present trends and insights derived from citizen interactions. Government officials can access dashboards displaying the frequency of issues and sentiment trends.
